About Us

ICM Properties, Inc. is a full service professional property management and investment company focusing on multi-family residential properties. We currently manage over two thousand apartment units on the North Side of Chicago

Established in 1966, ICM has steadily grown to become one of the largest residential management companies on the North Side of Chicago. With two fully staffed offices, our professional property managers, knowledgeable leasing consultants, and hardworking maintenance team are at your disposal.

ICM is not a rental agency. We manage every unit and you will deal directly with our company for all your needs. Our offices are close to all our buildings, making it easy to visit seven days a week.
